FAQ

Your Home Care Questions Answered

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and care options.

What services does Ruta's Homecare provide? +
We offer two main services: Ruta's Home (a residential group-home with 24/7 care) and Ruta's In-Home Care (personal care services delivered in your own home). Our services include bathing, dressing, grooming, meal preparation, medication reminders, companionship, and much more.
How do I know if home care is right for my loved one? +
Home care is ideal for individuals who need assistance with daily activities but want to maintain their independence and stay in a familiar environment. We offer free consultations to assess needs and recommend the best care options for your situation.
Are your caregivers trained and certified? +
Yes, all our caregivers are professionally trained, background-checked, and committed to ongoing education. We ensure our team meets the highest standards of care and professionalism while embodying our Christ-centered values.
What makes Ruta's Homecare different? +
Our Christ-centered approach sets us apart. We don't just provide care—we build relationships rooted in faith, compassion, and dignity. Every client is treated like family, and we draw strength from Philippians 4:13 to serve with excellence and love.
How do I get started with your services? +
Getting started is easy! Contact us by phone at (616) 589-7682 or (616) 272-8967, or email us at Clemanzu@gmail.com. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs and create a personalized care plan.
Do you provide care 24/7? +
Yes! Ruta's Home offers 24/7 residential care with round-the-clock supervision. For our in-home care services, we can arrange flexible scheduling including overnight care based on your specific needs.
